Stone Paper Scissors

Player: 0

Computer: 0

Instructions for Playing:

        
  •  Objective: The objective of the game is to defeat the computer by selecting a winning choice (rock, paper, or scissors).
    • 
         
         Game Interface:
         
         
  • When you open the game page, you'll see a heading "Rock Paper Scissors".
    • 
         
  • Below the heading, there will be three buttons labeled "Rock", "Paper", and "Scissors".
    • 
         
  • Click on one of the buttons to make your choice.
    • 
         How to Play:
         
        
  •  Click on one of the buttons labeled "Rock", "Paper", or "Scissors" to make your choice.
    • 
        
  •  The computer will randomly select its choice (rock, paper, or scissors).
    • 
         
  • The game will determine the winner based on the choices made by you and the computer.
    • 
         
  • The result of the game (whether you win, lose, or tie) will be displayed below the buttons.
    • 
         Restarting the Game:
         
       
  •   After each game, you can click on the buttons again to make a new choice and play another round.
    • 
         Have Fun!:
         
         
  • Enjoy playing the game and see if you can outsmart the computer by selecting the winning choice.
